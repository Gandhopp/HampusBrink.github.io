This game was a group project for an assignment while
I was studying at Forsbergs over the span of 2 months

    My contributions:

  • Enemy combat system
  • Boss combat system
  • Spells
  • See-through Wall Shader

The combat system is dynamic and has properties like rock paper siscors, The player has 3 attacks: blocking,light-attacks and heavy-attacks. Depending on what attack you do the interactions between enemies are different. You can also perform a parry if you block at the right time

The boss system was designed to be inherited and used for all future bosses, However this was the only one that made it in to the game as the others were scrapped. State machines were used to achieve this behaviour.
